Welcome! Enjoy your stay!

Howdy! I’m OgloTheNerd! I’m a Linux (and sometimes BSD) user and developer of free and open-source software (FOSS). I was raised outside Austin Texas, and my family has a nice chunk of land where we raise chickens and grow plants! I am best known for my YouTube channel “OgloTheNerd” and some popular projects like Rebos. My favorite programming language is C, specifically C99. I am also a very strong advocate for offline-first design and non-political software, hence why I don’t program in Rust anymore. I am also a very social person, so feel free to shoot me an email or ping me on one of the many chat platforms I use! Anyway, stay safe out there, and have fun living life! :)
