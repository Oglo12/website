Welcome! Enjoy your stay!

Howdy! I’m OgloTheNerd! I’m a Linux (and sometimes BSD) user and developer of free and open source software! I was born and raised in Austin Texas, and my family has a nice chunk of land where we raise chickens and grow herbs! I am best known for my YouTube channel “OgloTheNerd” and some popular programs like Rebos. I used to program in Rust, but now I primarily program in C. C is amazing… I love it so much! I do not regret my decision to stop programming in Rust. I stopped programming in Rust because it has a toxic community, a sketchy foundation and a “build system” (if you can even call it that) that downloads dependencies at compile-time instead of clone-time! C is also just a lot more fun to program in. I am also the kind of person who loves being pinged in places like Discord, so feel free to ping me in my Discord server or send me an email to start a conversation! I’d love to chat! Anyway, have a great day, and stay safe out there! Enjoy life! <3
